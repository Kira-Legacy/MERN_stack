Recipe: Indomine Noodles

Ingredients Needed:

  • Indomine Noodle
  • 4 glasses of water

structions:

  1. Boil the water
  2. Add the Indomine Noodles
  3. Put salt and spice (optional) to the cooking Indomine
  4. Wait for 3 minutes
  5. Your Idomine is ready. Enjoy!
